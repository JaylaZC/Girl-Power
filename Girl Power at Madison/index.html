Welcome to Shirley's Bakery

Established for over 70 years, Shirley's Bakery showcases a range of speciality cakes, cookies, and other yummies. Our products are ideal for entertaining guests, a family treat or a little self indulgence. Our full team of chefs and friendly staff take pride in providing attentive service while discussing your needs for your special treat and satisfaction.

The food story

Shirley makes cookies, cupcakes, cakes for all occasions including weddings, and candy including a sugar-free selection. Everything is made from scratch and when she makes buttercream frosting every week.

Cakes come in a variety of flavors including chocolate, vanilla, strawberry, lemon, red velvet, German chocolate, orange, coconut and if they have time, they will try to accommodate special orders.

Sweets From The Heart

In addition to the decorated variety, Shirley's also offers raisin rocks, chocolate macaroon, butter pecan, chocolate dipped pecan sandies and the ever popular wedding cookies rolled in powdered sugar.Wedding cakes are multi-tiered and the groom's cakes are available in shapes and decorations.
